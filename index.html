I am currently a Research Scientist at Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data (SRIBD). Before that, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen) (CUHKSZ), from 2019 to 2020. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from Xidian University in 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Hongwei Liu and Prof. Zhi-Quan Luo. From 2015 to 2018, I was a visiting Ph.D. student at CUHKSZ. I received the B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Xidian University in 2013.
